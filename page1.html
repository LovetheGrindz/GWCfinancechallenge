Smart Funds: Information

⊹ What is Smart Funds? ⊹

Smart Funds is an AI powered tool that provides users with advice and financial assistance tailored to each individual.

Smart Funds: Benefits

Smart Funds is designed for any individual regardless of their background. This tool has a plethora of features set just for you!

Exclusive but not limited to:

Expense Tracking

Provides expense tracking in which offers budgeting features that will track your spending habits, relay potential saving areas, and set realistic budgeting depending on your income

Debt management

Options for debt management for users with any amount of debt in which Smart Funds provides guidance on debt repayment and possible loan takeouts

Investment

Offers investment strategies in which uses AI algorithms to recommend investment areas and investment goals while considering past investments made

Smart Funds: Data Usage

Concerned on how our AI Tool
use will your data?

With consented permission, Smart Funds takes your income, expenses, and credit score to help you to make better financial decisions. This data is crucial for our algroithms to produce the best advice based on your situation. Those with limited access to services can be recommended financial advice empowering them to achieve personal financial goals.
