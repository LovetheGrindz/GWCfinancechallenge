Smart Funds: Backstory

Incite

Artificial intelligence has had a major impact in today's society, so Smart Funds puts it to use with its extensive possibilities. With many individuals struggling to make important decisions regarding their money, Smart Funds reduces the stress by providing countless information depending on one's position. With advice on investing and managing debt, individuals can alleviate the pressure of overwhelming fear and put time into more valuable parts of life. Smart Funds is directed towards a young audience who has little to no background on financial information or experience and can lay out a set plan to reach a desired goal. However, this isn't limited to those and is inclusive to all individuals. Recognizing the need for accessible and personalized financial advice is the goal for Smart Funds. This is crucial for those who lack access to such services and need a helping hand.

Curious on how Smart Funds runs with the use of artificial intelligence?

With the plethora of features we offer, each one has their own unique purpose and structure!

⟡ Adaptation ⟡

Smart Funds adapts to the markets conditions and changes advice based on certain circumstances while continuously learning from transactions and past events to cater to an individual to suggest personalized budgets

⟡ Investment ⟡

Analyzes market trends and relays investment opportunities to those who opt in by recommending strategies for individuals to reach their investment goals and informs individuals while managing risk

⟡ Debt ⟡

Examines debt and interest rates to formulate a personalized debt payoff plan(s) all while maintaining efficiently and saving on possible interest costs and has options of accelerated payment methods depending on needs

Reflection Questions

How does your project use AI to help people with their finances in a way that hasn't been done before?

  As stated in the previous sections, Smart Funds takes individuals past transactions and generates budgeting plans to cater to one's goals. Along with that, those with student loans or credit card debt can create payment plans that fit within their budget while still affording necessities. Because this tool is personalized, the use of AI is crucial by taking other's experiences and advice from financial advisors to give individuals effective financial advice/plans. To add on, AI is a tool that need to be fed information and can expand over time, leading to better responses. The constantly updated advice will allow users to keep up with the market (important for investors) which can more accessible to wider audience. AI has not been utilized in these areas, at least to this extent specifically towards individuals. Financial AI generated tools gravitate towards companies at the moment, but reaching individuals is important to improve financial literacy along with effective budgeting.

Describe a challenge you faced. How did you overcome it? How did you reach out for help?

  During this project, I endured a plethora of adversities one of which was sizing. I had made my project using the measurement root em for every element. However when I tested my website on mobile, the sizing was off and I had to redo almost every page. After watching tutorials and looking at online forms, I was able to fix my page after a couple of days. I do not know others around me who make websites so I felt alone in this process.

What did you learn from this process that will impact the way you interact with technology in the future?

  As stated before, I had no help with this website except stack overflow and other platforms. This was my first ever website I created using html, let alone css. I had to learn css and some html to have a layout and spend hours getting it the way I wanted. In the future, I will try not to jump into projects with no background and test the language with small activities to understand future projects better. This way, I'll get the best results and hopefully have those around me to help.

Why did you choose this format for your project?

  I knew sooner or later I will need to make a website so I decided to make one displaying what a AI finance tool would look like. I found that this will introduce myself into html and css that can be later used for other projects. Overall, I enjoyed this challenge even with its ups and downs and would choose to make a website again if I had the choice to.
